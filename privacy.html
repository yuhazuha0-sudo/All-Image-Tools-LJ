Privacy Policy — All Image Tools

Updated: November 19, 2025

All Image Tools is a simple, privacy-first web tool that performs image compression entirely in your browser. This page explains what data (if any) we collect and how we handle it.

1. Local / Client-side Processing

All image processing (compression, resizing, format conversion) happens on your device in the browser. We do not upload images to our servers. Your images remain on your device unless you explicitly choose to share or upload them yourself.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No images or files uploaded to our servers by the site.
  • No personal files, photos, or documents are stored by us.
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected by default.

3. Analytics & Third-Party Services

We may add third-party services (for example, Google Analytics) to understand usage patterns. If enabled, these services may collect anonymous usage data (page views, events like “compress” or “download”), but they will not receive or track your image files. You can request these analytics be disabled by contacting the site owner.

4. Cookies

The site does not require cookies for core compression functionality. If we add analytics or other optional features in future, cookies may be used according to those services' policies.

5. Security

Because all processing is client-side, the main security considerations are your device and browser. Avoid using untrusted public devices for processing sensitive images. We recommend keeping your browser up-to-date.

6. Links to Other Sites

This site may contain links (Donate, Help, About). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites. Please review third-party site policies before sharing information.

7. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or wish to request removal of any data (if ever stored), contact the site owner at: yuhazuha0@gmail.com.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When changes are made, the updated date will appear at the top of this page.
